# E-Commerce Glossary

## LTV (Lifetime Value)
Total revenue generated by a single customer across all their orders.
In this dataset: `SUM(oi.price)` grouped by `customer_id` via orders → order_items join.
Note: Olist dataset has mostly one-time buyers, so LTV ≈ first order value for most customers.

## Conversion Rate
Ratio of delivered orders to total orders placed.
`COUNT(*) FILTER (WHERE status = 'delivered') / COUNT(*)` from orders table.

## Churn
A customer is considered churned if they have not placed an order in the last 6 months.
Olist data spans 2016–2018.

## Seller Performance
Measured by: revenue generated, average review score, number of orders fulfilled, on-time delivery rate.
On-time = `delivered_at <= estimated_delivery_date` in the orders table.

## Payment Installments
Brazilian customers often pay in installments (`payment_installments` in payments table).
High installment count (>6) may indicate lower-income buyers or high-value purchases.

## boleto
A Brazilian payment method — a bank slip paid at a bank or lottery. Common for unbanked customers.
In the payments table: `payment_type = 'boleto'`.
